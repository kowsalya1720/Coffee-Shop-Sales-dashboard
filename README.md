# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales & Revenue Analysis Using Power BI

## 📌 Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze coffee shop sales data using Power BI and create an interactive dashboard that provides insights into sales performance, revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance. The dashboard enables stakeholders to make data-driven business decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📂 Data Source
**Dataset:** Coffee Shop Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transaction-level sales records, including:
- Transaction Date
- Product Name
- Product Category
- Unit Price
- Quantity Sold
- Total Sales
- Store Location

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

- Cleaned missing and duplicate values.
- Formatted date columns for accurate time-series analysis.
- Created calculated columns and measures using DAX.
- Calculated Total Sales (Quantity × Unit Price).
- Created Month, Day, and Year fields for trend analysis.
- Organized data into product, category, and location dimensions.
- Built measures for revenue, quantity sold, and average sales.

---

## 📊 Power BI Dashboard Design

### 🔹 Page 1: Sales Overview
- KPI Cards displaying:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Quantity Sold
  - Average Daily Sales
- Bar Chart showing Revenue by Product Category.
- Column Chart displaying Top Selling Products.
- Store Location-wise Sales Analysis.
- Date Slicer for interactive filtering by day, month, and year.

---

### 🔹 Page 2: Revenue & Performance Analysis
- Donut Chart showing Revenue Distribution by Product Category.
- Stacked Column Chart displaying Monthly Revenue Trends.
- Line Chart tracking Daily Sales Performance.
- KPI Cards highlighting:
  - Top Selling Product
  - Best Performing Store
- Interactive filters for product category and store location.

---

### 🔹 Page 3: Product & Customer Insights
- Detailed Sales Table including:
  - Product Name
  - Unit Price
  - Quantity Sold
  - Total Revenue
- Treemap comparing product sales performance.
- Region/Store Filter for location-based analysis.
- Product-wise sales comparison for business insights.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified the highest revenue-generating products.
- Compared sales performance across different store locations.
- Analyzed monthly and daily sales trends.
- Evaluated category-wise revenue contribution.
- Enabled interactive filtering for detailed business analysis.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel (Data Source)

---

## ✅ Conclusion

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the coffee shop's sales and revenue performance. It enables users to monitor sales trends, identify top-performing products and stores, analyze customer purchasing patterns, and support strategic business decisions through interactive data visualization.

The dashboard is suitable for:
- Business Performance Monitoring
- Sales Analysis
- Product Performance Evaluation
- Revenue Tracking
- Data-Driven Decision Making
