☕ Coffee Shop Sales & Revenue Analysis Using Power BI

📌 Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze sales performance and revenue trends of a coffee shop using Power BI. The dashboard helps business stakeholders track daily sales, identify best-selling products, and evaluate performance by category, region, and time.

📂 Data Source
Dataset: Coffee Shop Sales Dataset (uploaded .pbix file)

Content: Includes transaction-level data such as date, product name, category, unit price, quantity sold, revenue, and location.

🧹 Data Preparation
   -Cleaned missing or duplicate values.
   -Formatted date fields for time-based analysis.
   -Calculated new columns:

Total Sales = Quantity × Unit Price

Profit margins and percentage KPIs.

Created aggregated tables for:

Product Category

Store Region

Time-based metrics (Day, Month, Year)

📊 Power BI Dashboard Design
🔹 Page 1: Sales Overview
KPIs:

Total Revenue

Total Quantity Sold

Average Daily Sales

Bar & Column Charts: Show revenue by:

Product Category

Product Name

Store Location

Date Slicer: Enables dynamic filtering of daily, monthly, or yearly data.

🔹 Page 2: Profitability & Performance
Donut Charts: Revenue and profit share by product category.

Stacked Column Chart: Monthly sales trend.

Line Chart: Daily revenue trend.

Card Visuals: Display top-selling products and top-performing stores.

🔹 Page 3: Customer & Product Insights
Table View: Detailed sales report including:

Product name, unit price, quantity sold, total sales

Treemap: Visual comparison of sales by product.

Region Filter: Allows user to see sales insights based on store region or location.

✅ Conclusion
This Power BI dashboard delivers clear, actionable insights into the coffee shop’s business performance. It allows stakeholders to:

Track revenue and sales trends.

Identify best- and worst-performing products.

Make data-driven decisions on inventory, pricing, and promotions.

Monitor regional or store-level performance easily.
